Cultural Significance

The cultural significance of Nagaland jewellery lies in its deep connection to tribal heritage, identity, and craftsmanship. Traditional Naga ornaments, crafted from beads, shells, ivory, and boar tusks, symbolize status, bravery, and social identity. Each tribe, such as the Konyak, Ao, and Angami, has distinct jewellery styles reflecting their customs and beliefs. Worn during festivals and ceremonies, these vibrant adornments preserve Nagaland’s rich cultural legacy across generations.
